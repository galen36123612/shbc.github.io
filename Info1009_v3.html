About Us

Founded in 2023, Schweitzer Health Biotech Company Ltd. (SHBC) focuses on the health supplement industry and has launched an O2O platform. Offline, we collaborate with Asia's leading distributor, DKSH, to place our products in supermarkets and drugstores, while online we integrate AI technology with KOLs to promote our brand and build a comprehensive on-line to off-line sales network.

SHBC shareholders come from renowned companies in the biotechnology and internet technology sectors, with over 40 years of industry experience. The company emphasizes independent product development, with a focus on meeting consumer needs from formula design and production to distribution and sales. Its flagship product, "WEIDER Probiotics," originates from an 85-year-old American brand, known for its three core benefits: immediate effectiveness, long-term effectiveness, and proven results. It has become one of the top ten best-selling products in major American retail chains.

In 2024, SHBC pioneered the launch of Asia's first "AI Nutritionist," bringing new commercial value to KOL traffic and creating fresh development opportunities for the health supplement industry.
